About

Greetings, my name is Taylor, and I'm thrilled to have you here! Music and visual arts are my lifelong passions. As a neuro-divergent artist, I've taken a unique path, holding an English degree with a Math minor and working as a computer engineer to fund my art. I rekindled my love for music in 2019 as an excuse to sing, but it didn't become serious until 2020, during the global pandemic, when I started taking classical and jazz guitar lessons again. My music spans diverse styles, so there's something for everyone. Thanks for giving my independent music a listen. I'm deeply grateful for your support!
